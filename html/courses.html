Eligibility Criteria (UG & PG) of Computer Applications

Minimum requirement of the various courses

Diploma in Computer Application

  • No entrance test is required to get a Diploma in Computer Applications.
  • Candidates are selected on the basis of their marks in class 12th.

Undergraduate Computer Application Courses

  • Candidates must have scored an aggregate of minimum 50-55% in 10+2.
  • Aspirants can hail from any stream with English as one of their subjects in Class 12. However, some colleges mandatorily require having studied science subjects in class 12th.

Postgraduate Computer Application Courses

  • Candidates must have a graduation in Computer applications, Computer science or any other related field with a minimum of 50% marks or a GPA of 5/10 at the undergraduate level.
  • A minimum of 50-55% in Class 12.

Phd in Computer Application

  • Candidates must have completed their Masters in Computer applications, computer science or any other related field without any pending backlog with 50-55%.
